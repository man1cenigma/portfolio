About Me

My Journey as a Developer

Background

My journey as a developer began with a curiosity for how things work behind the scenes on the web. Starting with the fundamentals of HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, I built my own portfolio website from scratch, learning the importance of clean design, responsive layouts, and user experience.

Education & Training

As my skills grew, I dove into C# and software engineering principles through a structured bootcamp. I tackled a wide range of projects, from simple variable and math demos to more complex applications involving arrays, exception handling, and object-oriented programming. Each project challenged me to think critically, debug efficiently, and write maintainable code.

Technical Skills

Through hands-on practice with conditionals, loops, and methods, I developed a strong foundation in problem-solving and logical thinking. My later work with encapsulation and abstraction in C# helped me appreciate the power of modular, reusable code. I've learned to approach challenges with creativity and persistence, always striving to improve both my technical and soft skills.

Looking Forward

Today, I am confident building both web and desktop applications, and I am excited to keep learning and growing as a developer. I look forward to taking on new projects that push my abilities and allow me to create meaningful, user-focused solutions. My goal is to contribute to innovative projects that make a real difference in people's lives.
